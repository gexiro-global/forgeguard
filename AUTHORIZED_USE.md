# Authorized Use

ForgeGuard by Gexiro is for defensive posture self-checks.

You may use ForgeGuard only against instances you own or are explicitly authorized to assess.

You must not use ForgeGuard for internet-wide discovery, third-party probing, or activity that exceeds your authorization.

You must not modify ForgeGuard to request package blobs, manifests, or other protected artifacts from third-party systems.

Do not treat ForgeGuard output as proof of compromise without independent verification. ForgeGuard v0.2 performs posture inference, not exploitation.
